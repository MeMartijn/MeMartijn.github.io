University of Amsterdam: Interaction design

Interaction design is a course at the University of Amsterdam. During four weeks, I designed a couple of small assignments.

  • Week 1

  • Science Park

    Group assignment

    Case: A building at Science Park has an empty wall. The management of the University of Amsterdam would like to have something interactive on the wall. Think about something that fits into the environment and that is appealing to the audience.
    In this group assignment, our team thought about options that were appealing to the people that visit Science Park. A big part of the audience are students. Our solution was a screen with the departure times of the public transport on Science Park, because most students use public transport to get to and from Science Park. To make it interactive, we added a live Twitter feed.

  • WhatsApp

    Individual assignment

    Case: Choose a product that you use on a daily basis, and analyse your use as the 'user'.
    I chose the popular app WhatsApp, that I use for daily communication with friends and family. I analyzed my use of the app using UML and wrote some user stories.

    Download as PDF

  • Live healthy

    Individual assignment

    Case: Create a concept for a digital product that helps people to live healthier.
    Using user personas and a customer journey, I mapped the possibilities and chances for a succesful product. The analysis provided me with ideas for three apps: an app that provides the user with tips for healthy meals, an app that gives advice for healthy activities according to the weather forecast, and an app that can help you get rid of an appetite for snacks.

    Our team created a fairly simple design, with as the main purpose selling the book. We tried to accomplish that by placing a call-to-action in the center of the website.

    Group assignment

    The main aim of this assignment was to get used to user testing and get some experience in planning and carrying out tests. For this particular test, we asked our subjects to examine the popular Dutch website NPO.nl. We also offered some solutions to the problems that we discovered.

  • Week 4

  • Salary payment

    Group assignment

    Case: Redesign your own salary report to make it more understandable. Use user stories to guide your design decisions.
    This last group exercise was quite an easy one, to be honest. To redesign a salary report, we decided what information was the most important, and adjusted the layout of the report according to our thoughts.

  • Pie creator

    Individual assignment

    Case: Create a form for making custom pies, and focus on the right user inputs.
    This exercise was focused on forms and user input. I created a form to create a custom pie. The hard part of this exercise was to find the right input methods for each type of data that was needed. For example, to decide between a chocolate flavoured filling or a vanilla flavoured filling, what type of user input would be the best?
